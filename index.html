About Us

Building Sustainable Value in the Caribbean

Founded in 2018, CaricomInvest is dedicated to identifying, investing in, and nurturing high-potential businesses across the Caribbean region. We combine deep local expertise with global perspectives to create exceptional value for our investors and portfolio companies.

Our investment approach is characterized by discipline, rigor, and a commitment to sustainable development. We partner with management teams who share our vision for growth and operational excellence.

Investment Focus

Tourism & Hospitality

We invest in innovative hospitality concepts, boutique resorts, and sustainable tourism infrastructure that enhance the Caribbean's competitive advantage while preserving its natural beauty.

Renewable Energy

We support projects in solar, wind, and other renewable energy sources that reduce the region's dependence on imported fossil fuels and build climate resilience.

Agribusiness & Food Security

We invest in agricultural innovation, food processing, and supply chain improvements that enhance food security and create export opportunities for Caribbean producers.

Digital Infrastructure

We focus on technological solutions that overcome the challenges of island economies, from fintech innovations to remote work infrastructure and digital services.

Real Estate

We invest in premium commercial and residential developments across the Caribbean, focusing on properties that meet international standards while embracing the unique character of each location.
